Project Description
 We are designing a household managing program. It will allow house members to better coordinate with each other in terms of events, tasks, finances and more. Users will sign in using their Google accounts. Users will create a House and add house members to it, from their each member can log their expenses, events, and tasks. This info will then be presented to the user in a meaningful way.
